About Sherazade

Movement has been her language since age three.

From rhythmic gymnastics champion to kinesiology expert, Sherazade Mami brings athletic discipline and body science into every session.

Her work is built for clients who want more than a workout. Each session is shaped around strength, posture, control, flexibility, and the mechanics that help the body move well.

Philosophy

Precision, motivation, and care.

Athletic discipline

Years of high-level movement training inform Shera's eye for alignment, control, and graceful effort.

Scientific understanding

Kinesiology and body mechanics guide sessions that respect how each client moves, compensates, and improves.

Personal attention

Clients are guided toward their best physical shape with workouts adjusted to their goals and comfort level.
